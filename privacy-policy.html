This privacy policy outlines the actions that will be taken by Hrdavec Entertainment to ensure the safety of all of its users in terms of collecting, storing and managing the personal information its users have provided us during the course of the application (“app” or “game”).

To all new users of this game,
Before using, ensure you read our privacy policy in full, understand and accept the terms that have been created for this app.

If you do not want our games to collect, manage or use your personal information, you must a) not disclose any personal information throughout the game or b) discontinue using any service we provide, including deleting the app and all applications associated with Hrdavec Entertainment. If you do agree to use our games or applications, you must also understand that Hrdavec Entertainment does not hold any of your personal information, as we use third parties to optimise your experience.

Hrdavec Entertainment may change this privacy policy from time to time, so it is important for users to regularly check for any changes being made to this privacy policy. By continuing to use this game, Hrdavec Entertainment will assume you have read and understand the obligations set out in this Privacy Policy.
Collection of information (including third party users)

Hrdavec Entertainments third parties collect user data, therefore will personalize your experience to optimize your gameplay, will save and associate your data back so you can always access it through your game, create analytics and reports for other third parties (including Unity Ads) and most importantly to detect and defend your data against fraud. To store our user information, we use third parties such as Playfab to store all of our user gameplay, user IDs including Google login for player Authentication (Android), Game Center login for player authentication (iOS), optimising UI, SDKs, usernames, first and last names, email addresses and most importantly to optimize our users experience.

Hrdavec Entertainment also uses third parties to apply In App Purchasing to its games to help users unlock items to help increase and help them in their gameplay. If users do not wish to use In App Purchasing, please do not feel obligated to do so, or if you think that someone is using your account to make in-app purchases, Hrdavec Entertainment is not responsible nor does it have access to your personal information.  

Our games also include ads, mainly to help users unlock or achieve things faster than normal. This includes watching ads to unlock items, increase your level in the game and to mainly optimize your experience. At this time, we cannot disable ads and have no function to disable the ads for users.

Children under the recommended age

Hrdavec Entertainment does not collect or retain information from anyone under the age of 13, therefore we recommend that children under the age of 13 not play any of our games. We recommend this to ensure the safety of our user’s personal information, including their names, user ID, address and email addresses. If in the event Hrdavec Entertainments third parties has accidently collected personal information from users under the recommended age, we will endeavour to delete the information provided as quickly as possible
